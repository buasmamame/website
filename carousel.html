Vision

In a world where we always put our children before ourselves, we believe that we must be egoistic and prioritize our own time and relaxation, so we can be a role model for our children, because only by loving and caring for ourselves can we love somebody else.

 

Mission

Our mission is to help mothers of young children, enhance their health and self-love by addressing the issue of exhaustion and lack of personal time.


 

Objectives

Research Objective

At the end of 11th of April 2023, we want to reach 250 people on Instagram.

At the end of 11th of April in 2023, we want to reach 300 people on Facebook.

Affect Objective

At the end of 11th of April 2023, we want our target audience to feel egoistic about their self-love and we aim to convenience our target to put themselves first. We want our values to be recognized by the parents: responsibility/learning, relaxation/self-respect, self-love and egoism.

Response Objective

At the end of 11th of April in 2023, we want 250 women to follow our social media account and website.

  • 250 views on posts
  • 200 views on Reels
  • 50 likes on social media accounts
  • 5 comments or responses on our contents/posts
The Communication Message should achieve that the target audience has a clear overview about the Values, Vision and the Mission in our media platforms. By social media planning we create brand awareness and bring visitors into loyal clients and brand lovers. The goal will be measured by the analysis of each social media account and by the responses of the target audience; usage of hashtags, the views on the reels and the amount of followers, likes and comments on each post. According to the time limit we have decided to reach a small amount of target till 11th of April in 2023. Since the amount is reachable, we are focusing on loyal customers to ascertain the value of the relationship between the brand and the customers.
