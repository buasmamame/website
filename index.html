In a world where we, as mothers of young children, always put our children before ourselves, we believe that we must be egoistic and prioritize our own time and relaxation, so we can be a rolemodel for our children, because only by loving and caring for ourselves we can love somebody else.

Our mission is to help mothers of young children, enhance their health and self-love by addressing the issue of exhaustion and lack of personal time.
